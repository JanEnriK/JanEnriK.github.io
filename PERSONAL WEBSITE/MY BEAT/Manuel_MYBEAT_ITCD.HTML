Original Pilipino Music(OPM)

Unang Sayaw by NOBITA

Artist
- Nobita comprises Jaeson Felismino on vocals and acoustic guitar, Sam Aquino on lead guitars, Mark Quintero on bass, Lester Monegas on drums, and Richmond Bancolita on keyboards. The band made it to the semi-finals of the prestigious battle of the bands, "Muziklaban," and considered their loss a "blessing in disguise." They didn't stop creating and releasing music after the competition. Finally, They broke into the 2020 Pinoy MYX Countdown with their original, "Vie." Next came Ikaw Lang, then another hit single, "Unang Sayaw," and a contract with Sony Music Philippines in 2021.

Paraluman by Adie

Artist
- Adie Garcia uploaded videos of his cover songs on Youtube and immediately garnered thousand of views. In 2020, he signed up as an artist of O/C Records of singer Kean Cipriano. His single Paraluman was a big hit with more than 3.5 million views on its first month alone. It also made it to No. 1 in the Viral Top-50 on Spotify. Written and directed by Bela Padilla, the official music video features another social media sensation Ivana Alawi.

Binibini by Zack Tabudlo

Artist
- Zack Tabudlo was born on December 6, 2000 he is a Filipino singer-songwriter, musician and producer. He was first seen in The Voice Kids Philippines season 1 as a member of Team Bamboo. He later began his musical career in 2018 before he rose to fame in 2020 when he joined MCA Music/Universal through sublabel Island Records Philippines. His debut single "Nangangamba" was released in 2020. His song "Binibini", released in 2021, broke a record as the top local song on Spotify's Philippines charts.

Alternative/Indie

Break my heart again by FINNEAS

Artist
- Finneas Baird O'Connell was born on July 30, 1997, he is known mononymously as Finneas. He is an American singer-songwriter, record producer, and actor. He has written and produced music for various artists, including his sister, Billie Eilish. He has won eight Grammy Awards, including Album of the Year, Producer of the Year, Non-Classical and the Best Engineered Album, Non-Classical for his work on Eilish's debut album 'When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?'. Finneas has released several singles as a solo artist, and his debut EP, Blood Harmony, was released in October 2019. The EP includes "Let's Fall in Love for the Night", his most successful song to date, peaking at number 17 on the US Billboard Alternative Songs chart. His debut studio album Optimist was released through Interscope Records on October 15, 2021.

Happiness by Rex Orange County

Artist
- Alexander James O'Connor was born on 4 May 1998, he is known professionally as Rex Orange County, is an English singer and songwriter. He rose to prominence in 2017 following multiple features on Tyler, the Creator’s Grammy-nominated album Flower Boy. NPR Music journalist Zoë Jones has described his music as "a bright blend of hip-hop, jazz, and bedroom pop”. He has released one mixtape, Bcos U Will Never B Free, and three studio albums, Apricot Princess, Pony, and Who Cares? as well as a live album titled Live at Radio City Music Hall.

Ocean Eyes by Billie Eilish

Artist
- Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O’Connell was born on December 18, 2001, Los Angeles, California, U.S. She is an american singer-songwriter who first gained recognition in 2015 for the song “Ocean Eyes” and became the youngest person ever to win a Grammy for album of the year in 2020. In 2015 Eilish’s elder brother, Finneas Baird O’Connell, who performs under the moniker FINNEAS, enlisted her to sing “Ocean Eyes,” a song he had written and produced for his band at the time and later put online. The song quickly gained success, and Eilish signed with the boutique label Darkroom in the summer of 2016.

Chill Rhythm and Blues

American teen by Khalid

Artist
- Khalid is a non-traditional R&B singer and songwriter and he has attracted a large following with an undemonstrative but heartfelt vocal style and a down-to-earth perspective. Although he attained a major-label contract within months of uploading his first songs, "Location" moved into the mainstream at a slow tempo similar to that of the song itself and became a Top 20 pop hit just after the release of American Teen in 2017, one of the biggest album debuts of the late 2010s. Within a period of just over a year, Khalid went from an unknown everyday teenager to a chart-topping, platinum-certified pop star with Grammy nominations for "Location" and American Teen.

La La Lost You bt NIKI

Artist
- She was born and raised in Jakarta, Indonesia, Nicole Zefanya aka “NIKI” grew up around 90s R&B artists like Destiny’s Child and Aaliyah who influenced her music. She first gained popularity through her YouTube covers and original songs which have now been deleted. Prior to joining 88Rising in 2017, she independently released two songs namely “Polaroid Boy” and “Anaheim”. At present, she is dubbed as “the Internet’s favorite R&B princess” breaking the cliches of teen stardom by writing, recording, and producing her own music from the four corners of her bedroom.

Leave the door open by Bruno Mars

Artist
- Bruno Mars, by name of Peter Gene Hernandez, was born on October 8, 1985, Honolulu, Hawaii, U.S. He is an american singer and songwriter who was known for both his catchy pop music—which often featured upbeat lyrics, blended different genres, and had a retro quality—and his energetic live performances. He was nicknamed Bruno when he was a toddler and began entertaining in Honolulu at the age of four as an Elvis Presley impersonator with his parents’ band, the Love Notes. As a young teenager, he appeared in a local revue impersonating Michael Jackson, and he taught himself to play piano, guitar, bass, and percussion. After graduating from high school, he moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career as a musician.
